Ganesh Pooja

Lord Ganesh is every devotee’s favourite god. Ganesh Pooja is done almost on all occasions. This Pooja is done to remove all the obstacles and bring happiness in one’s life.

KEY INSIGHTS

Lord Ganesh is worshipped in this Pooja. Devotees pray for prosperity and wellbeing towards Lord Ganesh. This Pooja is done before starting any business/exam and other occasions. Durva and ashta dravya is offered to Lord Ganesh.

PROMISE

Our pandits are certified by authorized Vedic teaching institute. Pooja is done as per Vedic procedures. The Pooja material used is pure and genuine. We are punctual and supportive during the Pooja time period.

DESCRIPTION

Lord Ganesh ji is known to take away all the sorrow and grief from their devotee’s life. Therefore all the devotees perform the Ganesh Pooja in order to bring positivity and wellbeing into their lives. Ganesh Pooja is also performed before any major occasion. Lord Ganesh always listens to their devotee’s prayers.

Ganesh Pooja is performed by expert pandit. Idol or portrait of Lord Ganesh is decorated with flowers and Durva and ashta dravya is also offered in front of it. The Pooja starts with Ganesh Mantra and then ends with Ganesh Aarti. After the Pooja all devotees seek blessings from Lord Ganesh. The Muhurtha for this Pooja is decided by pandit on the basis of tithi, Nakshatra, and yog.
